About

The Smart Data Hack is running as part of Innovative Learning Week in Informatics at the University of Edinburgh.

We hope to encourage partnerships between local businesses, community organisations and student developers, and promote the use of Open Data for creative, socially empowering applications.

The twitter hashtag for the event is #ilwhack.
